Privacy Policy

Virotikly Automation • Updated: April 13, 2026
Section 01

Data Collection

Virotikly collects minimal data necessary to provide automation services. This includes authentication tokens for TikTok integration and video metadata provided during the upload process. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Section 02

TikTok API Integration

Our app utilizes the official TikTok API. By using Virotikly, you authorize us to manage your content based on the permissions granted during the OAuth authentication process. We only access the scopes necessary to post and schedule your content.

Section 03

Security & Storage

We prioritize the security of your assets. Video files processed via our "Local Upload" are handled through secure channels, and cloud integration URLs are accessed only for the purpose of transmission to your social channels.

Section 04

User Rights

You retain full ownership of your content. You may revoke Virotikly's access to your TikTok account at any time through your social media security settings or by contacting our support team.
